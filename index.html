Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Anna Piekarz ("developer", "we", "our", "us") operates the Monely mobile application ("App") on iOS and Android. We take your privacy seriously. The App is designed to work entirely on your device without requiring an account or transmitting your personal data to us or any third party.

1. No Account Required

Monely does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. You can use all features of the App anonymously.

2. Data You Enter — Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you create in the App is stored exclusively on your device using the operating system's local storage (Android SharedPreferences / iOS NSUserDefaults). This includes:

  • Expenses — amount, category, optional description, and date/time
  • Savings goals — goal name, target amount, deposits, and deadlines
  • Recurring expenses — name, category, amount, frequency, and date range
  • Reminders — name, title, optional description, and schedule
  • App settings — currency, monthly spending limit, language, date format, and first day of week
  • Subscription status — whether Premium is active and the expiry date (cached locally)

None of this data is transmitted to us or any external server. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up through your phone's own backup system (e.g. iCloud or Google One).

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or any other contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to us
  • Any data from your contacts, camera, microphone, or photo library

4. Notifications

The App can schedule local notifications for reminders you create. These notifications are generated entirely on your device by the operating system — no data is sent to any server to deliver them. The App will ask for your permission before scheduling notifications. You can withdraw this permission at any time in your device's Settings.

5. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Monely offers an optional Premium subscription (monthly or yearly) and a 7-day free trial. Payments are processed exclusively by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment card details or billing information. The transaction is handled entirely by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

After a successful purchase or trial activation, the App stores only a local flag indicating Premium status and the subscription expiry date on your device. No payment details are retained by us.

6. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications — to deliver reminders you create. Required only if you use the Reminders feature. You can disable this permission in your device's Settings at any time.

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photo library, or any other sensitive device resources.

7. Analytics and Third-Party Tracking

Monely does not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, advertising networks, or third-party tracking libraries. We do not track your behavior within the App and we do not build user profiles. No data is shared with third parties for advertising or any other commercial purpose.

8. Sale of Personal Information (CCPA)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party, as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) or any other applicable law. Since we do not collect personal information in the first place, there is nothing to sell.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR (EEA / UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data.
  • Right to restriction — you can ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object — you can object to our processing of your data.

Because Monely does not collect or store any personal data on our servers, these rights are exercised directly on your own device. All data is under your full control at all times. To delete all data, use Settings → Reset all data within the App or simply uninstall it. For any questions, contact us at the address below.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the App collects no personal data at all, there is no special risk to minors beyond what applies to any general-audience app. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address it promptly.

11. Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time directly within the App via Settings → Reset all data. Uninstalling the App will also remove all locally stored data from your device. Since we hold no data on our servers, no additional action is required on our end.

12. Data Retention

All data is retained solely on your device for as long as the App is installed. We do not retain any data on remote servers. Once you delete data within the App or uninstall the App, it is permanently removed from your device.

13. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's own security mechanisms (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We do not transmit your data over the internet and therefore there is no server-side security risk associated with using this App.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
