Privacy Policy - Monivo

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Monivo mobile application ("App", "we", "our") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Data You Create in the App

All financial data you enter in the App - including expenses, savings goals, recurring expenses, reminders, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using the operating system's local storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers, because we do not operate any servers.

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location data
  • Your device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any analytics or usage data
  • Any crash reports

3. Notifications

The App can send local notifications to remind you of upcoming expenses or savings goals. These notifications are generated entirely on your device by the operating system. No notification content is transmitted to any server.

To use this feature, the App requests permission to send notifications. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device Settings.

4. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Monivo offers an optional premium subscription ("Monivo Premium") via in-app purchase.

4a. Apple App Store

Payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment card details or billing information. Apple's Privacy Policy applies to all payment transactions: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4b. RevenueCat

To manage subscription status, validate purchases, and deliver premium features, the App uses RevenueCat (Revenue Cat, Inc.). When you make or restore a purchase, RevenueCat receives:

  • A purchase receipt from Apple (to verify your subscription)
  • An anonymous app user identifier (generated automatically, not linked to your identity)
  • Your subscription status (active, expired, cancelled, etc.)
  • Basic technical information: app version, operating system version, and country as reported by the App Store

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email address, payment card details, or any financial data you enter in the App.

RevenueCat's Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5. No Advertising or Analytics

Monivo does not integrate any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or behavioral tracking libraries. We do not track how you use the App, and we do not share any data with advertisers.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties, except as described in Section 4 (Apple App Store and RevenueCat), which is necessary solely to process your subscription.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored on your device and is under your full control at all times.

  • You can delete all data within the App via Settings → Reset all data
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device
  • To request deletion of subscription-related data held by RevenueCat, please contact us at annapiekarz93@gmail.com and we will submit the request on your behalf

8. Children's Privacy

Monivo is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights (EEA, UK, and California)

Since the App stores data locally on your device, you have direct and full control over it at all times. For any subscription-related data processed by RevenueCat, you may:

  • Request access to data held about you
  • Request deletion of that data
  • Object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at annapiekarz93@gmail.com.

10. Security

App data is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We recommend keeping your device software up to date to benefit from the latest security protections.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
